Spicy Garlic Prawn Pasta


  • SERVING 2
  • PREPPING TIME: 5
  • COOKING TIME: 30 MIN
  • CUISINE: ENGLISH, ITALIAN

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g linguine or spaghetti
  • 25g butter
  • 200g raw peeled prawn, preferably tiger prawns
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 100ml white wine
  • squeeze lemon juice
  • small handful flat-leaf parsley, roughly chopped

    • METHOD

    1. Cook the pasta in a large pan of boiling, salted water according to pack instructions.

    2. Meanwhile, heat a small knob of the butter in a frying pan. When it starts to sizzle, add the prawns and fry for 1 min until they start to change colour.

    3. Add the garlic and sizzle for 1 min more, splash in the wine, then bring to the boil.

    4. Swirl in the rest of the butter, season with salt and pepper and a squeeze of lemon juice, then stir in the chopped parsley.

    5. When the pasta is just cooked, drain and toss through the prawns. Divide the pasta between 2 bowls, pour over any excess sauce and serve straightaway.
